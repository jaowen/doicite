\title{Example use of \texttt{doicite}}
\author{Jeremy A. Owen}

\begin{document}
\maketitle

With this package, you can create citations by simply specifying digital object identifiers (DOIs) in your source file. For instance, we can cite a book  \doi{10.1119/1.19136} and an article \doi{10.1103/PhysRevE.64.026118}. You may have to run \LaTeX\ and BibTeX more than once on your documents to get all the citations to appear.
